Mirage Game Studios

Mirage Game Studios is a game development company located in sunny Karlstad, Sweden.
The company was founded by four avid gamers with roots in the area during fall of 2016 in collaboration with THQ Nordic. The team as well as the game has grown quite a bit since then, both in scope and capabilities.

About Us

With a combined career 15 years in the game industry, we put emphasis on 'game feel' and high quality assets while working closely with developers to implement the right audio envisioned for each project.

We work full-time in Game Design and Programming in Gothenburg and Karlstad, Sweden. This allows us to offer help with implementation of all audio & logic.

Titan Sky Audio started as a way for us to work on passion projects on the side of our everyday jobs. We saw a lack of quality in the sound design in a lot of indie titles and wanted to provide a unique, high quality, soundscape for any type of game out there.
